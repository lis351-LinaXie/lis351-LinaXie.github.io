How to Make Eraser Carving?

Some Tools:

  1. Carving Knife
  2. Eraser
  3. Pencil
  4. Sulfuric acid paper
  5. Pizzicato
  6. Print Clay
Carving Knife:
A small and sharp carving knife is essential for making detailed cuts in the eraser.
Eraser:
The eraser should be made of a soft and flexible material, such as rubber, for ease of carving.
Pencil:
Use a pencil to sketch out your design on the eraser before carving.
Sulfuric acid paper:
Sulfuric acid paper is used to transfer your design from paper onto the eraser.
Pizzicato:
Pizzicato is a type of needle tool used for creating fine details and texture in the eraser carving.
Print Clay:
Print clay is used to make prints of your finished eraser carving.

Warning: Eraser carving can be dangerous and should be done with caution. Always use appropriate safety gear, such as gloves and eye protection, and work in a well-ventilated area.
