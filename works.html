Embroidery

Embroidery handwork is a timeless art form that allows for the creation of intricate and beautiful designs on fabric using various stitching techniques.

Carving

Carving involves the use of sharp tools to create intricate designs and patterns on wood, stone, or other materials, requiring patience, skill, and a steady hand.

Pieced Beans

Pieced beans involves arranging and stitching together small beans of different colors and sizes to create intricate and colorful designs.
